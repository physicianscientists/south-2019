APSA is coming to Houston!

Register now for the Southern Regional Meeting of the American Physician Scientists Association!

Register Now!


Registration deadline is September 20th!

Join us in the world's largest medical center!

The Baylor College of Medicine MSTP will host the 2018 APSA Southern Regional Meeting in Houston, TX on Saturday, September 29, 2018. Registration is open to current and prospective medical students and dual-degree candidates, as well as residents, fellows, and faculty. The meeting will include several keynote scientific and career development talks, student posters and oral presentations from many different institutions and departments, and breakout sessions on topics relevant to multiple stages of training. Check out the schedule here.

We look forward to seeing you there!

Parking

The best parking garage for the APSA Southern Regional Meeting is TMC Garage 15. Take the elevator down to the first floor and cross into the connected MD Anderson UTHealth Graduate School. We will have signage and conference personnel there to greet & direct you further.

Alternatively, you may also find additional parking in TMC Garages 2 and 6. These are only a short 5 minute walk from the MD Anderson UTHealth Graduate School.
